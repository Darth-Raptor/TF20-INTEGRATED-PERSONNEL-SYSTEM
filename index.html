Standards

We are aiming for reliable members, not just interested applicants.

TF20 expects members to communicate clearly, maintain modpack and platform readiness, respect attendance expectations, and operate as part of a chain of command. We are not chasing “hardcore” theater for its own sake. The goal is consistent execution, not empty roleplay.

Maturity

Own your commitments, communicate early, and handle feedback like an adult.

Preparedness

Show up with working mods, working comms, and enough initiative to solve simple issues.

Team Discipline

Follow leadership, support your element, and understand that reliability matters more than ego.

Schedule and expectations

Operations run best when everyone knows the rhythm.

1. Screening

Join through Discord, connect your account, and complete the application with enough detail for real review.

2. Recruit intake

Accepted applicants are moved into the roster, linked to their Discord identity, and brought into the training pipeline.

3. Training and integration

Recruits are brought through orientation, baseline expectations, and unit familiarization before broader assignment.

4. Operational tempo

Attendance, LOA, and personnel visibility are tracked inside the portal so current members are supported, not lost in spreadsheets.
